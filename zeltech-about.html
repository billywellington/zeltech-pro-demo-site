About Zeltech Pro

Who We Are

Zeltech Pro is a professional hygiene, cleaning, and pest control company based in Strand, Cape Town, proudly serving clients across the Western Cape — including Stellenbosch, Somerset West, Gordon's Bay, Bellville, Durbanville, and surrounding areas. With over five years of industry experience, we support businesses and residential clients with reliable products and expert services designed to keep environments clean, safe, and compliant.

Our offering spans hygiene solutions, consumables, industrial cleaning chemicals, professional cleaning services, and pest control — allowing our clients to work with one trusted partner for all their cleanliness and maintenance needs. Every solution we provide is practical, cost-effective, and tailored to the unique requirements of each space.

With over five years of 5-star client ratings on Google, we've built our reputation on quality, reliability, and attention to detail. From once-off deep cleans and end-of-tenancy cleaning to ongoing product supply and scheduled maintenance, Zeltech Pro is committed to delivering consistent results and dependable support across the Western Cape.
