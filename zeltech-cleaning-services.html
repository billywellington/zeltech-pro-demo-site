Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer once-off and recurring cleaning services?

Yes. We offer both once-off services such as deep cleaning and post-construction cleaning, as well as scheduled recurring cleaning plans for homes and businesses.

Are your cleaning staff insured and bonded?

Yes, all of our cleaning professionals are thoroughly vetted, background-checked, insured, and bonded for your complete peace of mind.

How do you determine pricing?

Our pricing is based on the size of your space, the type of cleaning required, the frequency of service, and any special requests or additional services needed.

Can I customise my cleaning service?

Absolutely. We offer fully customisable cleaning plans tailored specifically to your requirements and preferences.

Do you provide your own cleaning products and equipment?

Yes. Our teams arrive fully equipped with professional, eco-friendly cleaning products and tools suitable for each environment.

Are your cleaning methods safe for people and pets?

Yes. We use safe, responsible cleaning solutions and follow best practices to protect people, pets, and indoor environments.
