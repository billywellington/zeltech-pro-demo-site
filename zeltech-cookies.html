Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and browsing behaviour.

2. How We Use Cookies

Zeltech Pro uses cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyse website traffic
  • Understand how visitors interact with our website

Cookies may store anonymous identifiers such as your IP address or browser type.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, including page navigation, security features, and form submissions. These cookies cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. They allow us to measure website traffic, improve performance, and analyse visitor behaviour. Example tools may include Google Analytics.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be used to display relevant advertisements or track marketing campaigns. They are only used if consent is given.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block cookies
  • Delete stored cookies
  • Receive alerts before cookies are stored

Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Consent

When you first visit our website, you may see a cookie banner asking for your consent to use cookies. You can accept, decline, or customise your preferences.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
